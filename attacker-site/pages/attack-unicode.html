Macronutrients

Your body needs three main macronutrients: proteins, carbohydrates, and fats. Each plays a vital role in your health.

Proteins are essential for building and repairing tissues. Good sources include lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

Carbohydrates provide energy for your body. Choose complex carbs like whole grains, vegetables, and fruits over simple sugars.

Fats are necessary for hormone production and nutrient absorption. Focus on healthy fats from sources like avocados, nuts, and olive oil.

Practical Tips

  • Eat plenty of vegetables and fruits
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars
  • Stay hydrated with water
  • Practice mindful eating

Consult a healthcare professional before making significant dietary changes.
