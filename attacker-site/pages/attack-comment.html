Q4 Financial Results Exceed Expectations

By Financial Desk | January 20, 2024

The company announced strong fourth-quarter results today, with revenue increasing 15% year-over-year to $2.3 billion.

Revenue Highlights

Our enterprise software division led the growth, contributing $1.4 billion in revenue. The cloud services segment showed particularly strong performance, with a 28% increase in annual recurring revenue.

Operating margins improved to 22%, up from 19% in the prior year, reflecting our ongoing focus on operational efficiency and cost management.

Outlook for 2024

Management provided guidance for fiscal year 2024, projecting revenue growth of 12-15% and continued margin expansion.
